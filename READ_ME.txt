Final Fantasy V (SNES) - Alternate Field Sprites (with English v2.1)

04 JUL 2026	

If you like me played the GBA/Advance saw that the new character portraits inspired by Amano's art look nothing like the field sprites and were bothered by it.

Well look no further this hack is for you!

With this hack the vast majority of character sprites from the main team to important side characters like King Tycoon, Kelger, and the Black Chocobo to name a few were modified to represent a more Promotional/Amano art look.

(Due to the nature of the hack with palette swap editing some NPCs and other field sprites from items to enemies were modified from their original look too, some more than others to appear more organic to the game setting.)

- Graphics altered to look more like promotional/Amano's art.
- Hack uses the English v2.1 - Final Fantasy V: Legend of the Crystals translation by team Spoonify93 

v1.0
--------------------------------------
*Player overworld sprites - Bartz, Lenna, Galuf, Faris, and Krylle have been modified to look mainly like their Character Portraits seen in the FFV - Advance GBA inspired by Amano. (due to limited palette colors characters Bartz and Krylle look more like their SNES Non-Amano art but is more fitting for their GBA Portraits.)

*Palette Colors Green, Pink, and Purple have been modified and the majority of sprites that use those colors have been affected and some have been modified- Characters: Men NPCs, Women NPCs, King of Wales, Queen of Kardak, Ghido the turtle, Cid, The Black Chocobo, Moogles, Enemy field sprites like Siren has an example, the 12 weapons, Dragon Grass, Plant enemies, and Etc...

*End Game Faris - Sarisa Form has been modified to have more detailed and has been given an extra eyeball to be consistent with her new pirate sprite.

*Galuf while resisting Exdeath has also been modified to incorporate new sprites and palette change.

*Other Characters have been modified to also look like their Amano / GBA Character Portrait Art - Exdeath, King Tycoon, Kelger.

*The intro cinematic sprites and the ending cinematic were not modified. (because I couldn't find the sprites)

----------------------------------------

How to apply patch:
Acquire a rom of FF5|JP (preferably legally and yours), this patch, and an IPS patching program (Lunar IPS seems to be the standard).

Run the program, and apply the patch towards your japanese ROM of FF5.
Enjoy!

- Lunar IPS
